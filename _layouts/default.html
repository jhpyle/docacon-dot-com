About Docacon

Docacon is the annual meeting of the docassemble user community.

Docacon 2021 was a virtual meetup using the Remo platform. In the first part, we had a chance to connect with other docassemble users at virtual "tables" in Remo. The second part consisted of a series of plenary presentations by a variety of docassemble users who demonstrate some things they have been working on. Then we reconvened at "tables" for some follow-up discussion.

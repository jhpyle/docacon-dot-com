About Docacon

Docacon is the conference of the docassemble user community.

Docacon 2023 will take place at Suffolk University Law School in Boston, MA on Tuesday, April 4, 2023 as an add-on conference to LITCon 2023.

The conference will take place at:

Suffolk University Law School
5th Floor

Increase your docassemble skills

Docacon 2020 is a one-day intensive training conference with two tracks: beginner and advanced. You can self-select into which track is appropriate for you.

The beginner track is appropriate for people who have no or a little experience with docassembe.

The advanced track is appropriate for people who are already developing guided interviews in docassemble.

The topics covered in each track are currently in development. You can provide input into the topics covered by filling out this survey. You can wait until the day of the event to decide which track you want to attend.

Registration cost: free. If you enjoy using open-source software and attending free conferences, consider making a donation to the Merlin Legal Open Source Foundation to help sustain this and other open-source projects.
